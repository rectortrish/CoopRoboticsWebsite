First LEGO League

Each year, FIRST® provides teams around the world with an annual challenge that is based on a set of real-world problems that scientists and engineers are facing today. FLL Challenge teams will work together to complete the challenges two parts: the Robot Game and the Project. Co-Op Robotics' FLL Team is accepting inquiries for the 2023 robotics competition season for participants in grades 4th - 8th.
