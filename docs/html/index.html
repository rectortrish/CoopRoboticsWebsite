Co-op Robotics offers interactive LEGO Education robotics and programming classes to the homeschooling community for children ages 6-14. Students learn pseudo-coding, coding, robotics, and engineering principles, as well as how to work cooperatively with partners and small teams to achieve goals.

All classes are rooted in respect, teamwork, and collaboration. Students learn key social skills in group and partner-pair decision making, negotiation and compromise and improve cooperation and teamwork skills.
